## Currently supported

- Pullio

## Trigger options

![trigger-channels.png](../../assets/screenshots/integrations/hotio/trigger-channels.png)

1. Triggers
    - `Pullio` - Enable notifications from Pullio
1. Channel
    - Pick the channel on your server to send Pullio notifications to.
